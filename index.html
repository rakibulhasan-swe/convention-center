If a Have Ya Herd event is canceled you will be notified by email and/or phone call as soon as possible prior to the event, and will receive a full refund or credit toward a future event.

Have Ya Herd is not responsible if registered participants appear for a canceled event after a failed attempt has been made to notify them.

If you cancel less than 4 business days before your scheduled event, NO REFUNDS OR RESCHEDULES will be provided. If you cancel more than 4 business days before your scheduled event, a FULL REFUND OR A RESCHEDULE will be provided.
